About Me!

I'm Rylin, a artist, collector, and photographer! I like to draw art mainly revolving some of my favorite characters and I do have OC's as well but I dont draw them as often as I should. If you're interested in checking out some of my work, go to the Gallery Tab and check out my stuff! There is also going to be a tab for some nature photos that I've taken that I wanna share, but I havent gotten to it yet. (I AM VERY SLOW AT UPDATING... I am more of a ideas person...)

Site Goals

This site is mainly a place to be a archive for my art, the things that I like, and Windows Modding, the last thing being something I am very passionate about and hope to document well.

I don't have a exact vision for the content on the site but I know that the things listed are the main things. I am still coming up with new ideas and this is the 3rd time I've redesigned my site, which this isnt really my favorite yet but if this works out then I think it'll be pretty good. One of the problems with the last site was that it did NOT work well on phones, but this one does way better. This one is significantly more organized than the previous, the older one was pretty jumbled together. I feel like though in this redesign and organization I pulled a "Modern Tech Company" move and made everything too simple, I plan to change that and make this site better with time.
